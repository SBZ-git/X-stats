Understanding Engagement

Engagement is calculated as the sum of all interactions with a post:

  • Views: Number of times the post was viewed
  • Quotes: Number of times the post was quoted
  • Replies: Number of direct replies to the post
  • Reposts: Number of times the post was reposted

For example, if a post has 100 views, 5 quotes, 3 replies, and 2 reposts, its total engagement would be 110.

This metric helps understand the overall reach and interaction level of posts, with higher numbers indicating more successful content.
